Meet Your Guide

Looking for someone that can help navigate you through life's ups and downs? Do you need a boost to set you back on your feet so you can show the world who's boss? My goal in therapy is to be your player 2 as we work together to unlock the best person you can be.

My passion is empowerment; teaching essential life and coping skills to help you navigate through life in a healthy way. I know you already have it in you, so it's time to help you discover that strength for yourself. You are not alone in this journey, so let's take that next step together.

I have over five years of experience working with children, teens/adolescents, and adults from all walks of life. I am also certified in Prepare and Enrich for my Pre-marital/Marital clients through my Master's program. Through these experiences I can provide a diverse insight for an enriching and well rounded experience.

If you have read this far, I want to congratulate you for taking this first step in wanting to become a healthier you. I know this can be a scary process of journeying into this unknown. I want you to know I'm proud of you for making it this far. If you're ready to take the next step or have any questions, please reach out for a free phone consultation.
